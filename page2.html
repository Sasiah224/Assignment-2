My Favorite Hiking Trails

This is a page all about the hiking trails that I like!

Trexler Nature Preserve

Trexler Nature Preserve is a beautiful hiking trail in Allentown, PA. It offers scenic views and diverse wildlife.

South Mountain Preserve

South Mountain Preserve is known for its challenging trails and stunning vistas. It's a favorite among experienced hikers.

Cedar Creek Park Trail

Cedar Creek Park Trail is a family-friendly trail that winds through lush greenery and offers plenty of spots for picnicking.
